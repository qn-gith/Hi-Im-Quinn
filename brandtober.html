Brandtober

During Brandtober, I dedicated 3-5 days to each design, from initial concept development to final execution. This process included brainstorming ideas, designing logos, creating brand identity elements, and designing packaging for both existing and conceptual brands.
It was an intensive but rewarding experience, allowing me to push my creative boundaries and refine my design skills.

This became my ritual (almost) every year.

ÉN KITCHEN

RESTAURANT
October 2023

Red and gold is a classic color combination known for stimulating the senses, evoking a sense of nostalgia for the target audience—Vietnamese customers.
The logo features the initial 'E', creatively illustrated as a frying pan or a food plate. The cursive 'E' adds a touch of elegance, conveying a high-end image and a promise of quality.

MISS MINH TATTOO

TATTOO SHOP
October 2023

Miss Minh Tattoo draws inspiration from traditional Vietnamese art, particularly the timeless beauty of Vietnamese women as portrayed in historical artworks.
The design merges classic elegance with a modern twist, capturing the essence of contemporary Vietnam nostalgia.
